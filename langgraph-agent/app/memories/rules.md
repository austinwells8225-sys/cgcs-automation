# CGCS Email Triage Rules

## Priority Classification

### High Priority
- Government officials (federal, state, local)
- ACC leadership and administration
- Complaints or urgent concerns
- Time-sensitive event requests (within 2 weeks)
- Follow-ups from approved reservations

### Medium Priority
- Standard event space reservation requests
- General questions about CGCS facilities
- Follow-ups on pending requests
- Community partner inquiries

### Low Priority
- General informational inquiries
- Non-urgent questions about policies
- Requests for future planning (3+ months out)

## Category Rules

### event_request
- Any email requesting to reserve or book event space
- Inquiries about availability for specific dates
- Requests to modify existing reservations

### question
- Questions about CGCS services, policies, pricing
- Questions about facility capabilities or equipment
- General "how do I..." inquiries

### complaint
- Negative feedback about past events or experiences
- Issues with facilities or equipment during events
- Billing disputes

### follow_up
- References to existing request IDs or previous conversations
- "Just checking on..." or "Any update on..." emails
- Responses to previous CGCS communications

### spam
- Marketing emails
- Unsolicited vendor pitches
- Phishing attempts
- Mass mailings not relevant to CGCS

### other
- Anything not fitting the above categories
- Internal ACC communications not event-related
